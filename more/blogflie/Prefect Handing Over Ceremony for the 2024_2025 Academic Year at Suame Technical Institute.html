May, 2024

The prefect handing over ceremony is a significant event at Suame Technical Institute, marking the transition of leadership and the continuation of student governance. As we approach the 2024/2025 academic year, this ceremony symbolizes not just the change of guard but also the values of leadership, responsibility, and community spirit that our institution upholds.

Significance of the Ceremony

The handing over ceremony serves several important purposes:

1. Transition of Leadership: It provides a formal platform for the outgoing prefect to pass on responsibilities to the newly elected prefect. This transition is crucial for maintaining continuity in leadership and ensuring that the incoming prefect is well-prepared for their role.
2. Acknowledgment of Contributions: The ceremony offers an opportunity to recognize the hard work and dedication of the outgoing prefect and their team. It’s a moment to celebrate their achievements and contributions to the school community.
3. Inspiration for New Leaders: For the new prefect, the ceremony is an occasion to share their vision and goals for the upcoming academic year. It inspires fellow students to engage and support their new leaders in fulfilling their duties.
4. Strengthening School Community: The event fosters a sense of unity among students, staff, and administration. It brings everyone together to celebrate leadership and commitment to the values of Suame Technical Institute.

Ceremony Program

The prefect handing over ceremony typically includes several key elements:

1. Opening Remarks: The ceremony will commence with a welcome address from the SRC President, highlighting the significance of the event and the importance of student leadership within the school.
2. Recognition of the Outgoing Prefect: The outgoing prefect will be invited to reflect on their tenure, sharing experiences, challenges, and successes. This segment allows them to express gratitude to their peers and mentors for their support.
3. Introduction of the Incoming Prefect: The newly elected prefect will be formally introduced. This includes a brief background on their achievements and qualifications, setting the stage for their leadership.
4. Handing Over of Responsibilities: A symbolic passing of responsibilities will take place. The outgoing prefect may present the new prefect with the school’s prefect badge, a document outlining duties, and any relevant materials.
5. Speech by the Incoming Prefect: The new prefect will have the opportunity to address the audience, sharing their vision, goals, and plans for the year ahead. This speech is pivotal in setting the tone for their leadership.
6. Closing Remarks: The ceremony will conclude with closing remarks from a faculty member, emphasizing the importance of student leadership and encouraging all students to support their new prefect.

Celebrating Leadership


The prefect handing over ceremony is not only a formal event but also a celebration of student leadership and commitment. It provides an opportunity for the entire Suame Technical Institute community to come together, fostering a spirit of collaboration and support for the incoming leadership.

Looking Ahead


As we transition into the 2024/2025 academic year, the prefect handing over ceremony sets the foundation for a new chapter in student governance. It is a reminder of the critical role that students play in shaping the culture and values of our institution.
In conclusion, we look forward to a memorable and impactful handing over ceremony that honors the contributions of the outgoing prefect while welcoming the new prefect with open arms. Together, let us embrace the spirit of leadership and strive for excellence in the coming year at Suame Technical Institute.
