Affiliations

KOICA World Friends

KOICA World Friends works closely with Suame Technical Institute to promote technical education through volunteer programs, financial support, and hands-on training. This affiliation enriches our students' learning experience and enhances our infrastructure. With KOICA’s support, we are better positioned to equip students with skills that meet global industry standards, preparing them to take on leadership roles in the workforce.


Planet One

Planet One’s partnership with Suame Technical Institute focuses on bringing cutting-edge technological solutions to our curriculum. By integrating modern technology and training resources, Planet One helps equip students with the skills needed to thrive in the tech-driven world. This collaboration also supports sustainable practices in education, ensuring that our students are prepared for the future.


German Cooperation Implemented by GIZ

GIZ’s partnership with Suame Technical Institute has significantly contributed to improving the quality of our vocational education programs. By providing expert trainers, funding for infrastructure, and aligning the curriculum with industry needs, GIZ ensures that our students receive high-quality technical training. This collaboration helps students develop the skills necessary to excel in their careers and contribute to national development.


Commission for Technical and Vocational Education and Training (CTVET)

CTVET works closely with Suame Technical Institute to ensure that our courses and certification programs meet the highest national and international standards. By providing accreditation and support for curriculum development, CTVET helps us maintain the quality of our technical programs. This affiliation ensures that our students graduate with the skills and credentials required to succeed in the competitive job market.


Ghana Education Service (GES)

Suame Technical Institute’s affiliation with GES enables us to align with the national educational framework and standards. With GES’s support, we ensure that our students receive comprehensive and high-quality education that is recognized both nationally and internationally. The GES partnership also facilitates the professional development of our faculty, enhancing the overall educational experience for all.


Ghana Technical and Vocational Education and Training (GTVET)

GTVET’s collaboration with Suame Technical Institute has been instrumental in developing and standardizing our technical and vocational education programs. With a focus on enhancing the skills of students and aligning our curriculum with industry demands, GTVET ensures that our graduates are highly qualified and ready to take on the challenges of the modern workforce.


National Vocational Training Institute (NVTI)

NVTI plays a crucial role in supporting Suame Technical Institute’s efforts to provide quality vocational training. By certifying our programs and ensuring they meet national standards, NVTI helps us offer internationally recognized qualifications that enhance our students' employability. Through this affiliation, we are able to equip students with practical skills that are highly valued in the job market.
